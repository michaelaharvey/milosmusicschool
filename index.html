Welcome to Milo's Music School

My name is Nicki, and I am a professional music teacher working for Capistrano Unified School District. In my free time, I offer private music and piano lessons in the Lake Forest and Mission Viejo areas.

Lessons

I offer lessons at my home, but I can also go to your home. Whichever works best for you. The price for my lessons is $40 for a half-hour lesson, or $75 for a one-hour lesson.

About

I learned to play the piano at age 9, and continued to play music throughout highschool and college in Jazz and Marching Band. I led the youth worship music program at Inland Hills Church, and I have been working as a music teacher for Capistrano Valley for 1 year. I have a passion for teaching, and will soon be graduating from Cal State Fullerton and pursuing my teaching credential.
